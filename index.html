Exploring College Basketball's Top 25

The visualization linked below was created to show the turbulence (and sometimes lack of) of each college basketball season from the 1970-71 season to the just finished 2013-14 season. It displays either the Associated Press poll or the Coaches Poll for each season, data availability permitting. All data was taken from:
ESPN
Sports-Reference.com
SportsLogos.net
NCAA Team Colors
Code samples/libraries used:
d3.js
jQuery
jQuery Cross-Domain-Ajax
d3-tip
to-title-case
Brush Snapping II
d3 n00b

Instructions for use

  1. Clicking the radio button will change the displayed poll to either the AP or Coaches poll
  2. Selecting a year from the drop-down will change the displayed poll to the AP or Coaches poll (whichever is previously selected) of the selected year.
  3. Clicking on any of these and any other lines in the graph, or the text labels on the right, will highlight that school and display circles at each week of the season. These circles can be hovered over to see the week and rank of that school.
  4. The week numbers can be clicked to update the column chart and table on the right side of the visualization.
  5. Clicking and dragging in between the numbers will zoom in on the selected weeks to offer a more in-depth look at the season.
